    Preparation

    01

    Place all dry ingredients in a bowl and mix together.

    02

    Add the wet ingredients to the dry and mix. The dough should come together into a ball. If it's too dry, add water one tablespoon at a time. If it's more like a batter, add flour one tablespoon at a time.

    03

    Remove the dough from the bowl and place it on a pizza tray. Portion into 4 oz pieces — each will produce one 8" grilled pizza.

    04

    Roll each portion into a ball and lightly coat with olive oil. The dough does not need to rise and is ready to use immediately.

    ❄
    Storage

    Dough can be refrigerated for 2–3 days or frozen for longer storage. Bring to room temperature before rolling out.

    Notes
    • Water temperature matters — keep it between 95°F and 115°F to properly activate the yeast.
    • Add water or flour adjustments in small amounts — a little goes a long way with dough consistency.
    • Lightly oiling the dough balls prevents them from drying out while you prep your toppings.
    • For grilling, stretch the dough thin, oil both sides, and grill directly on the grates over medium-high heat.
