Classic
Fudgy Brownies

Dense, intensely chocolatey, barely set in the middle. Melted semisweet chocolate folded with butter, eggs, and sugar — no shortcuts, no frills.

    Method

    1. 1
      Prep

      Preheat oven to 350 °F. Butter an 8-inch square baking pan.

    2. 2
      Melt

      Melt the chocolate and butter together in a saucepan over low heat, stirring until smooth. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.

    3. 3
      Mix

      Beat the eggs with the sugar until the sugar is mostly dissolved. Stir in the chocolate mixture, then fold in the flour, salt, vanilla, and nuts until just combined — don't overmix.

    4. 4
      Bake

      Pour into the prepared pan and spread evenly. Bake 25 minutes, until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs. Let cool completely before cutting.

    Notes

    • Pull them out when the center still looks barely set — they firm up dramatically as they cool.
    • The nuts are optional, but they add texture that keeps each bite interesting.
    • Let them cool fully before cutting or they'll crumble. An hour at room temperature does it.
