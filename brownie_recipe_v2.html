Classic Fudgy Brownies

Oven350 °F
Bake25 min
Pan8-inch square
Makes~9 brownies

Adjust batches

1 pan · ~9 brownies
    Method
    1. 1Preheat oven to 350 °F. Butter an 8-inch square baking pan.
    2. 2Melt the chocolate and butter together in a saucepan over low heat, stirring until smooth. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
    3. 3In a bowl, beat the eggs with the sugar until the sugar is mostly dissolved, about 2 minutes.
    4. 4Stir the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture. Then fold in the flour, salt, vanilla, and nuts until just combined — don't overmix.
    5. 5Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly. Bake for 25 minutes, until a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs. Let cool in the pan before cutting.
    For extra-fudgy brownies, pull them out on the earlier side — the center should look just barely set. They firm up as they cool.
