      Preparation

      01
      Make the Crust

      Mix the flour and salt together. Work in the margarine until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs, then add ice cold water one teaspoon at a time until the dough just comes together. Press into a 9" pie dish.

      02
      Make the Custard

      Combine all custard ingredients — sugar, corn syrup, margarine, lightly beaten eggs, vanilla, and salt — in a large bowl and mix well. Pour into the unbaked crust.

      03
      Top with Pecans & Bake

      Arrange the pecans on top of the custard filling. Bake at 350°F for 45–50 minutes, until the filling is set with just a slight wobble in the very centre. Cool completely before slicing.

      Liz's Notes from El Paso
      • Always use a little more pecans than the recipe says — pile them on top generously.
      • Same goes for the vanilla. A little extra goes a long way.
      • Ice cold water is the secret to a flaky crust — stick your water in the freezer for a few minutes first.
      • The pie is done when the edges are set but the very centre still has a slight jiggle.
      • Let it cool fully before cutting — it firms up beautifully as it rests.
